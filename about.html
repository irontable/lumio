Our Story

Made Slowly.
Made to Last.

Lumio began with a single question: why does sustainable home fragrance have to feel so ordinary?

Who We Are

A Small Studio in London

Lumio was founded in 2025 in a small kitchen in East London. We started by making candles for friends, and the response told us something: people were hungry for home fragrance that was both beautiful and honest about what it was made from.

Every product we make uses 100% natural soy wax sourced from renewable UK farms, cotton wicks, and fragrance oils certified free from phthalates, parabens, and synthetic musks. We produce in small batches — never more than we need — so nothing goes to waste.

We believe your home should smell like something that means something.

Our Values
