Our Story

Made Slowly.
Made to Last.

Lumio began in a small London kitchen with a single question: why does sustainable home fragrance have to look and feel so boring?

Every candle we make uses 100% natural soy wax sourced from renewable UK farms, cotton wicks, and fragrance oils that are free from phthalates and parabens. We produce in small batches — never more than we need — so nothing goes to waste.

We believe your home should smell beautiful without costing the planet anything.
